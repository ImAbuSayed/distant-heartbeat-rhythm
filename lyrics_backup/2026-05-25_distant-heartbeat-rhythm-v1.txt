[Hook]
[Upbeat acoustic strumming]
I feel the rhythm when you pull me close
You are the secret that I want the most
A gentle touch under the neon light

Your shadow dancing on the wooden floor

I am so lost inside your midnight haze

I just want you to stay right here

[Chorus]
[Modern country-pop lift]
